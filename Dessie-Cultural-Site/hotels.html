Golden-Gate Hotel
why choose us

Golden Gate Hotel comprises 46 rooms and is a 10-minute walk from Lake Hayq. You will be able to use free parking, an ATM, a newspaper stand onsite. This accommodation is in a 15-minute walk from Amanuel Church. Lalibela is 90 minutes’ drive away. The rooms are furnished with an individual safe, a mini-fridge-bar and a work desk. Rooms have bathrobes and towels. This venue offers on-site breakfast in the restaurant. The hotel provides meals, a special menu and a play area for families with children. Wireless internet is available in the hotel rooms for free.
