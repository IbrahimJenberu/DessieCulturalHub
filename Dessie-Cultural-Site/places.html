Awesome Places

Ayteyef Adarash

‘Ayiteyef Adarash’ is located in Dessie Town. It was built by King Michael in 1907 E.C. Before it got its present name from the king, the hall used to be known as ‘Giber Bet’ or Hall for Banquets.

Traditional Dressing

Around Dessie city, traditional dressing of the people is very different. Men wear beautiful traditional clothes with grace, and ladies wear pretty dresses to attract their future husbands.

Borena-Saint National Park

The Borena Sayint National Park is located 80 km from Dessie. There are more than ten caves where historical incidents once occurred, now part of the country's history.

Jema Ngus

Jema Nigus Mosque is located 37 km from Dessie. The great Muslim scholar, Haj Seid Mujahid, built the mosque in 1764. It is the place where the birthday of Prophet Mohammed, Mawlid, was celebrated for the first time in Ethiopia.
